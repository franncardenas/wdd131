Buenos Aires, Argentina

Argentina is the eighth-largest country in the world and the second-largest in South America. It spans diverse ecosystems, from the jungles of the north to the glaciers of Patagonia in the south. Home to iconic wildlife such as pumas, guanacos, Andean condors, and Magellanic penguins, it boasts rich biodiversity. Argentina is renowned for its pampas, Mendoza's vineyards, and the majestic Iguazu Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World. Culturally, it's famous for tango, soccer passion, and culinary staples like asado, empanadas, and mate.
